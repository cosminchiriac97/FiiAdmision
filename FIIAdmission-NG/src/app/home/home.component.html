What do our graduates say?

I graduated the Faculty of Computer Science of the "Alexandru Ioan Cuza" University in Iaşi. I consider myself, from this point of view, a privileged person because I had the honor of being educated by respected and open-minded professors while also having part of colleagues from whom I had tons to learn.

Through this institution I managed to fructify opportunities, opportunities which are needed and available to all the community of students... so with the best intentions I invite you to discover them yourselves!


Alexandru Pruteanu (graduate FII 2001), Microsoft România

Computer Science is not a field that cannot be merged with others. On the contrary, there are a lot of ways to do that. You can combine it with biology (bio-informatics), art (design, graphics), medicine (working with x-ray images), movie industry (special effects), economy (database, transaction security), physics (quantic informatics), and so many more..


Alexandrina Orzan (graduate FII 2003), team researcher at MAVERICK, INRIA, Rhône-Alpes
